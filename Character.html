Synopsis

Rio is a 2011 animated musical adventure-comedy film produced by Blue Sky Studios and directed by Carlos Saldanha. The story follows Blu, a domesticated macaw who learns to fly and embark on an adventure to Rio de Janeiro to find love and freedom.
